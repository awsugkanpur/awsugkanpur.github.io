Welcome to the AWS User Group Kanpur !

Welcome to the AWSUGKNP! Based in Kanpur, we are a community of AWS enthusiasts who come together to learn, share, and collaborate on all things AWS. Our group is dedicated to helping members grow their knowledge and skills in the AWS cloud, and to connecting with like-minded professionals in the local area.

Whether you are new to AWS or an experienced user, we have something for everyone. We host regular meetups, workshops, and conferences in Kanpur, and provide resources such as online courses and tutorials to help our members stay up-to-date on the latest developments in the AWS ecosystem.

If you are interested in joining our community, we welcome you to attend one of our events or get in touch with us through our social media channels. We look forward to meeting you and supporting you on your AWS journey in Kanpur!
